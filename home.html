Masterclass of DIY
«Look I did it»
It’s decided, I’ll get started! But because of the cost of labor, rather than hiring a company, I prefer to do the job myself. However, I need some guidance to organize myself and complete my project on my own.
The solution ? I call on Mon Coach Brico to take advantage of DIY classes at home. According to my needs, my coach, a building pro, comes to my place to advise me on tools, materials… and pass on his know-how to me. But on-the-job coaching doesn’t stop there! My Brico Coach also accompanies me with practical lessons throughout the duration of the work and corrects me when I make mistakes, without ever doing for me.
At the end of my project I saved 80% on the cost of labor and I can tell everyone: «Look, I did it»
